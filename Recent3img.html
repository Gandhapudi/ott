Acharya is an upcoming Indian Telugu-language action drama film[2] written and directed by Koratala Siva. It is produced by Matinee Entertainment and Konidela Production Company.


The film stars Chiranjeevi as the title character alongside Ram Charan, Kajal Aggarwal, and Pooja Hegde. It features music composed by Mani Sharma with cinematography. The film was launched in October 2019. Principal photography began in January 2020 but the production has been delayed owing to the COVID-19 pandemic.
